How to Play

After downloading the game and desired map, open c:\users\username\appdata\local\maker\maps\ for the map file.

Then open the game, type the maps name (ex: default.txt) and press MapLoader to play, or press MapMaker to edit the chosen map, also works with nonexistent files.
